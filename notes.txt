For the most part, graphs will only be generated for players with 3 or more data points to pull from.
Any Loss/Gain of Yardage on offense not due to passing or penalty is counted in each rushing related stat.
Rush Efficacy describes the ratio of efficient carries to overall attempts.
Efficient movements of the ball describe plays that advance the ball >= 5 yards or convert.
Return Yardage is not yet being tracked.
Kicking Points are not yet being tracked.
